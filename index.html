Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026
§ 01

Who We Are

PokerDesk – Home Game Manager ("the App") is developed and maintained by an individual developer operating under the name Obsayo.

Contact: oh.obsayo@gmail.com

§ 02

What This App Does

PokerDesk is an organisational tool for managing physical poker chip sets and home poker games. It is designed exclusively for offline, recreational use among friends and family.

PokerDesk does not support real-money gambling, online wagering, or any form of financial transaction between players.

§ 03

Data We Collect

PokerDesk collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected
  • No account registration is required or offered
  • No location data is collected
  • No device identifiers are transmitted
  • No analytics or crash-reporting services are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated
§ 04

Data Stored on Your Device

All application data is stored exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences and local storage. This includes:

  • Chip set configurations (colours, quantities, denominations)
  • Player names and game statistics
  • Blind structure profiles
  • App settings and preferences
  • Tournament logo image (if uploaded)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

§ 05

WiFi TV Feature

PokerDesk includes an optional WiFi TV feature that starts a local HTTP server on your home network. This allows the blind clock to be displayed on a TV browser.

  • The server operates exclusively on your local network (LAN)
  • No data is transmitted to the internet
  • No data is logged or stored by the server
  • The server is active only while the blind clock screen is open

The INTERNET permission declared in the app manifest is required solely to enable this local server functionality.

§ 06

Third-Party Services

PokerDesk uses the following third-party services:

Google Play Billing

Used to process in-app purchases (Premium upgrade). Google's own privacy policy applies to this transaction. We receive only confirmation of purchase status — no payment details.

AdMob (Free version only)

The free version of the app may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users see no advertisements.

§ 07

Children's Privacy

PokerDesk is rated 16+ and is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors. The app contains poker-themed content intended for adult recreational use only.

§ 08

Data Security

Since PokerDesk stores all data locally on your device and transmits nothing to external servers, the security of your data depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

§ 09

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All data is under your direct control on your device.

To delete all app data: go to Android Settings → Apps → PokerDesk → Clear Data, or simply uninstall the app.

§ 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

§ 11

Contact

oh.obsayo@gmail.com

Funkcja WiFi TV

PokerDesk zawiera opcjonalną funkcję WiFi TV, która uruchamia lokalny serwer HTTP w sieci domowej. Umożliwia to wyświetlanie zegara blindów w przeglądarce telewizora.

  • Serwer działa wyłącznie w lokalnej sieci (LAN)
  • Żadne dane nie są przesyłane do internetu
  • Żadne dane nie są logowane ani przechowywane przez serwer
  • Serwer jest aktywny tylko podczas otwartego ekranu zegara blindów

Uprawnienie INTERNET zadeklarowane w manifeście Aplikacji jest wymagane wyłącznie do obsługi tej lokalnej funkcji serwera.
